Explore Journey in Korea

If this is your first time planning a trip down under, these are the places in Korea I would suggest to see the highlights and make the most of your time traveling in Korea..

During my road trip around Korea, we met a lot of people visiting Korea for a short period, most of them on a tour. If you prefer to travel independently like me, travel is a big industry down under and it’s easy to go it alone.

I’m going to suggest a list of places to visit in korea, how long to stay in each location, what to see and do, even where to sleep at night. I’ll take a lot of the guesswork out for you.

Of course, I have to leave out some amazing places like Seoul and Jeju Province. I understand the desire to see as much as possible, particularly if you are visiting from the USA where most of you only have a standard two-week vacation

But Korea is a country mixed with cultural and moden. Can you imagine doing all of the US in three weeks? It’s impossible, especially when you add in flying and driving times.
