What was your favorite part of Jeju-do Island?

Jeju-do Island sightseeing isn’t just about the flora and fauna, though.

You can let your imagination run wild at the Trick Art Museum, or remember simpler days in childhood at the Teddy Bear Museum.

If you’re ready to enjoy a skin-crawling good time, pay a visit to the Psyche Butterfly Multipark to see insects in action.

Seoul the wild and beautiful place ...

Seoul, this intriguing destination boasts an innovative, forward-thinking culture but it’s also steeped in history. While traveling around this northwestern city, you’ll come across massive shopping complexes just on the outskirts of ancient temples and palaces.

Recommended Places to Visit: Jingfu Palace, Seoul Tower, Myeongdong, Bukchon Hanok Village, Insadong, Changdeokgung Palace, Nami Island, Lotte World, Everland, Namdaemun Market, Deoksugung Palace, Chengshan Sunrise Peak, Seoraksan National Park.
